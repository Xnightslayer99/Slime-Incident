The story:
for the non-demo it will be that you play as a slime and try to get your slime friends back from the giant red slime-eater

Target audience is children

Audio is techno (by camellia who just so happened to make a song with the same name)

Logo is the slime, The original character i created was also the slime (all sprites were made by me)

How to play:
WASD to move
berserker/archer have a left click
mage uses spacebar and c
archer also uses spacebar
berserker has a parry (right click)

heath can be obtained in the following ways:
1. leveling up
2. parrying a bullet with your sword

enemies are eliminated when their hp reaches 0, deal damage to them using your attacks

death is disabled for ease of grading
